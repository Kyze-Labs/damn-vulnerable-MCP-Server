# NovaTech Employee Onboarding Guide

## Welcome!
Welcome to NovaTech Solutions. This guide will help you get started.

## First Day
1. Set up your laptop (IT will provide)
2. Activate your @novatech.io email
3. Join #general and your department channel on Slack
4. Complete security awareness training

## Tools & Access
- GitHub: Request access from your manager
- AWS: Request via IT ticket (approval required)
- Jira: Auto-provisioned with your email
- Notion: Company wiki at notion.novatech.io
